Announcing Hola

Does your printer or other device on your Wi-Fi network have a web site?

Hola finds and displays all web sites on your Wi-Fi network, displaying them in-app or in Safari. Hola is a web browser for sites in a local Wi-Fi network advertised via the Bonjour (also called Zeroconf) protocol.

Respecting your privacy, Hola does not collect any information and is ad-free.
